About Me

Hello!

I am an undergraduate senior pursuing a Bachelors (BA) in Computer Science & Statistical Data Sciences, class of 2019. Computer Science is an art where every coder starts with a blank canvas and produce something so unique and personal to that person - having the freedom to create. In addition, Data Science allows us to gain deeper understanding on the subject matter that is fascinating to the individual, from Hiking Trails to Melanoma Cancer. I see Computer Science & Statistical Data Science working harmoniously, allowing us to learn and innovate the future.

Goals:

  • Use my problem solving skills and my attention to detail to gain exposure to new methodologies in the field of Computer Science and/or Data Sciences

  • Have the opportunity to be pushed, to collaborate, to be constantly inspired, to think critically, and to learn from brilliant and diverse minds

  • Use my skills to help the wellbeing of my community
